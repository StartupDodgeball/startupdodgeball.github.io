The Details

Great to see you. We'd love to see you join the global team of local leaders who make Startup Dodgeball Worldwide happen.

It is a straight-forward thing, it took just about 2 days to organize each event in BCN & BER 2013

What You Get

• An awesome day with great fun and excitement, no kidding

• The ideal base to touch base with the movers+shakers personally

• Associate you and/or your company with fun and excitement, not just boring biz talk

For Whom

• Made for local technology startups

• Startup folks, hipsters, nerds, creatives

What

• 6 hours in total + get-together afterwards

• 5 hour tournament play: Qualifying and single-elimination round

• One battle entails: 6 balls, 2x 6 players min, 10 max

• Whiteboard will be helpful for everyone to see e.g. status

• Tournament setup and rules will be provided

Where

• Played on an in/outdoor volleyball court, or at least on 16x8m

• Court shall be confined so that balls stay close to the court (not a 100% requirement, but outdoors kinda essential)

When

• Date and time is up to you

• Many other cities will take part

What We Do For You

• Help with outreach if doable

• Provide all invitation templates, papers, tournament rules + guidelines

• All sign-ups go through this site and ti.to's ticketing system

• If you need to pay a court, we'll wire the money to you

• We add all startups that play to the city page asap

Process

• Find a court (out- or indoors) and set a date

• Invite local startups and the community (use our template)

• Guide teams to this site to sign up

• Secure a local sponsor (if needed)

• Follow up 2x and update everyone close to the actual date

• Have an awesome tournament, this is guaranteed!

Your "home" on site will be CITY.startupdodgeball.net
